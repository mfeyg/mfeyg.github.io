Diana Feygelson is a designer based in Chicago. She studied sculpture at Michigan State University, and is currently pursuing a Masters of Design in Designed Objects at The School of the Art Institute of Chicago.

She applies creative problem-solving to design functional objects that play with users’ expectations. Her work calls attention to things that go unnoticed and explores new ways of looking at mundane actions.
