Ember

The Ember’s smooth metallic surface does not contain a switch, a pull chain, or any hidden buttons. It looks like an ordinary lamp, but it is lit and doused like a flame.

To turn the Ember on, shine a light on its shade. When you’re ready for darkness, lightly blow on the lamp to extinguish its light.

The working prototype is composed of an Arduino board, a light sensor, and an anemometer.

Dimensions: 20" x 10.5" x 10.5"
